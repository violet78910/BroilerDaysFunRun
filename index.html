Course:

The 5K race will start and end at the Wanek Center. It will loop over to Reit Lane; LEFT on County Road J; RIGHT onto Middle Road; TURN AROUND on Middle Road; RETURN to County J and run/walk back towards the Wanek Center on the bike path past the pool on Gavney Road.

Refreshments:

Water will be available at the turn around point on Middle Road (.77 from corner of County J and Reit Ln.). There will also be water and Gatorade available at the end.
